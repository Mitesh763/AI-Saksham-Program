  • apple

    • A tropical fruit that is high in vitamins A and C, fiber, and antioxidants.

    • A fruit that is rich in potassium, vitamin C, and dietary fiber. Bananas are long, curved fruits with a creamy white interior and yellow or brown skin.

    • A tropical fruit that is evergreen.

  • guava

    • A nutritious fruit that is rich in fiber, vitamins, and minerals.
